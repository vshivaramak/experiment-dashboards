Two Things the Model Couldn’t See
1 The RUX Experiment Moved Returning Customers to A&R
The Review Upsell Experience experiment targeted Returning customers, directing them toward A&R (assisted review) products. The model had no signal this was happening.

2 AI Is Shifting New Customers to DIY
74% of New customer volume is now TTO (DIY) products. This isn’t a blip — AI-powered tax tools are making first-time filers confident enough to file without assistance.

The model caught the direction but underestimated the speed. FY27 A&R targets will need to account for this structural shift.
